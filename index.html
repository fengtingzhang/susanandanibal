Flying in

By Airplane

The nearest international airport to the city of Salamanca is in Madrid.
Madrid–Barajas Airport is about 2 hours from the wedding venue in Salamanca, in the Castile and León region. It is not inside Madrid, from Madrid you should take a bus or a train to get to the wedding venue.

Ground transportation

From the airport, select a form of ground transportation

Either

By Bus

Madrid Aeropuerto <-> City of Salamanca

If you're not going through Madrid city center, take a direct bus from the Madrid airport to the city of Salamanca. Purchase your ticket from Avanza Bus, specify Madrid Aeropuerto as origin and Salamanca as destination.

Or

By Train

Madrid Chamartín <-> City of Salamanca

There is train from Madrid Chamartín Railway Station in Madrid city center to the city of Salamanca. Purchase your ticket from the national train company Renfe, specify Madrid-Chamartin as origin and Salamanca as destination
